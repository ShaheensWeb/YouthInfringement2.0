The annual Youth Infringement Festival is a creative outlet for young artists between the ages of 15 and 25 to create and perform live theatre in a professional setting. Directors, actors, writers and stage managers are picked from Ottawa youth to put together a unique showcase of theatre in Downtown Ottawa at the Arts Court Theatre.
