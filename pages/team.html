THE TEAM - Producers

Jeremy Piamonte, Senior Producer

Cory Hogan, Junior Producer

Hailey Masterson, Junior Producer

Jeremy Piamonte is a recent graduate of the Algonquin College Theatre Arts program. This is his fourth year with the festival, starting out as an actor in 2012, then directing in 2013, and finally producing for both the 2014 and 2015 years. Jeremy has worked primarily as an actor in Ottawa, but has also dabbled in directing, writing, producing, and sound design. He has had the pleasure of working with many fine artists both inside and outside of Youth Infringement, and he hopes that this year will bring many more fresh faces to the theatre.

Born and raised city boy Cory Hogan is excitedly taking part in the Youth Infringement Festival for his second year in a row. Cory is a second year student at the University of Ottawa studying an Honours specialization in Political Science with a minor in Theatre. He has tried his hand at a variety of activites however, has been in love with the art ever since he started acting at the age of four. From film to stage Cory has found enjoyment from entertaining and the experiences that come with it. Cory has a range of experiences that equip him with the experience for the task at hand as he aspires to help make this festival a memorable one and hopes everyone enjoys the shows!

Hailey Masterson is currently studying Theatre and Arts Administration at the University of Ottawa. She began her start at Youth Infringement by stage managing the 2013 runaway hit, "Final Sparkle Ascension". When not stage managing, Hailey is an avid theatre administrator, and has worked for the National Arts Centre, the Ottawa Fringe Festival, Odyssey Theatre, and the Great Canadian Theatre Company.
